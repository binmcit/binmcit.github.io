Welcome to My Webpage

Hi, I’m Bin Wu — a computer science student at the University of Pennsylvania, currently living in Philadelphia’s vibrant Gayborhood neighborhood.

Philadelphia is an incredibly walkable and diverse city, and I enjoy exploring its unique neighborhoods like Chinatown, South Philly’s Italian Market, and the Mexican community areas. I also love walking to the UPenn campus and along the scenic Schuylkill River trail.

This website is a place where I share a little about myself, my experiences, and some fun too! Feel free to explore and get to know me better!

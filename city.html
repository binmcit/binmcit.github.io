My Life in Philadelphia

Hello! I currently live in the Gayborhood area Philadelphia and I’m a computer science student at University of Pennsylvania.

Philadelphia is a very walkable city, and I love taking long walks through different neighborhoods. It’s one of my favorite ways to enjoy and explore the city.

My neighborhood: the Gayborhood

Beautiful UPenn Campus

One of the best things about Philly is its cultural diversity. I love visiting different neighborhoods such as:

  • Chinatown – Great food and a vibrant community
  • South Philadelphia – Home to Italian and Mexican neighborhoods
  • University City – Where I often walk to UPenn and enjoy the riverside

Exploring Chinatown

Relaxing by the riverside near UPenn

Philadelphia is more than just where I live and study — it’s a city that I’ve come to love through everyday walks, community vibes, and all the small discoveries I make while exploring on foot.
